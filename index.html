As for accessibility, the site did pretty well according to WebAIM, though it seemed like the website did not get scraped properly. There was only 1 error according to WebAIM. The site used aria labels and alt text for all images. The color contrast was also good, so I stuck with a similar color scheme.

Research

took inspiration from Amazon and Target

Takeaways

  • Amazon's emphasis on price integer
  • Amazon's price per unit
  • Target's two banners, with the sticky search bar
  • Target's usage of chips over large block of categories on left
  • Target's add to cart button to make purchasing faster
  • Target's locate store button since 99 Ranch Market is primarily brick and mortar
